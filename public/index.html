Fried Rice

Serves 2 | Around 15 Minutes | Allergens: Eggs

Ingredients:

3 cups short-grain rice, 2/3 cup chopped carrots, 1/2 cup peas, 1 clove garlic, 2 large eggs, 2-3 tbsp vegetable/neutral-tasting oil, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp MSG.

Method:

1 - Wash and cook the rice in a rice cooker. For best results and to avoid soggy/clumpy rice, cook rice a day in advance and refrigerate overnight.

2 - Put carrots and peas in a saucepan with water. Bring to a boil or until vegetables are cooked through, then immediately drain and set aside.

3 - Heat a wok over high heat, and add oil. Once oil is hot, add in vegetables with garlic and cook until fragrant. Add eggs and scramble to combine.

4 - Add in your pre-cooked rice, and stir. Use the back of the spatula to break rice clumps. Add soy sauce, MSG and stir quickly.

5 - Your fried rice is ready! Serve while hot.
